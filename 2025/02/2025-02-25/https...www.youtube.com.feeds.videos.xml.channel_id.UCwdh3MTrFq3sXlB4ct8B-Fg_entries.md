Emma has been a fan of Emperor’s Children for the last 18 years, and with the new Champions of Slaanesh box coming out, it was a struggle to keep her at bay. Watch her obsessively talk about her delusionally delightful journey through painting her army. 

Here is the painting guide for Emma’s Emperor’s Children army:

Black details:
Basecoat – Corvus Black
Drybrush – Dark Reaper
Highlight – Thunderhawk Blue

Purple details:
Basecoat – Kakophoni Purple
Shade – 1:3 Sigvald Burgundy & Contrast Medium
Highlight – Dechala Lilac

Green details:
Basecoat – Sybarite Green
Shade – 1:2 Aeldari Emerald & Contrast Medium
Highlight – Gauss Blaster Green

Metals:
Basecoats – Iron Hands Steel & Brass Scorpion
Shade – Druchii Violet
Highlight – Stormhost Silver

Skin:
Basecoat – Rakarth Flesh
Shade – 1:2 Reikland Fleshshade & Lahmian Medium

Bases:
Base texture – Armageddon Dust
Shade - Seraphim Sepia
Drybrush - Screaming Skull

Tools:
S Base
S Dry
S Layer
M Layer
