Shower niche shelf tips…I apply thin-set mortar to the top of tiles inside my niche. Then I carefully set the shelf. Be careful. It’s very easy to chip tiles when doing this. I ensure the shelf is level left to right. But I pitch it slightly (1/16” at most) so water drains toward the shower.
