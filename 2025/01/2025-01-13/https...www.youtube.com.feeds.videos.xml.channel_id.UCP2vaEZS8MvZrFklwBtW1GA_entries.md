Tile mortar removal tips…I ZipWall the door and tape the vent to contain dust. The SDS drill removes most of the mortar. I use a tile removal bit with it. The rasp on the multi-tool removes leftover mortar. Don’t forget to wear a silica dust respirator to protect your lungs 👍🏽🔥#demo #bathroominspiration #homerestoration #diybathroom
