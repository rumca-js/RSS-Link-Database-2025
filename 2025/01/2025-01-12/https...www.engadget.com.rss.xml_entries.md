## Awesome Games Done Quick raises another $2.5 million for a cancer nonprofit
 - [https://www.engadget.com/gaming/awesome-games-done-quick-raises-another-25-million-for-a-cancer-nonprofit-202228938.html?src=rss](https://www.engadget.com/gaming/awesome-games-done-quick-raises-another-25-million-for-a-cancer-nonprofit-202228938.html?src=rss)
 - RSS feed: $source
 - date published: 2025-01-12T20:22:28+00:00

<p>The weeklong speedrunning marathon Awesome Games Done Quick 2025 wrapped up in the wee hours of Sunday morning, and organizers say it managed to pull in a total of $2,556,305 for Prevent Cancer Foundation.&nbsp;</p>
<p>As could be expected, the event in Pittsburgh brought a good mix of skill and silliness: there was a <em>Crazy Taxi</em> speedrun featuring a live band; FunkopotamusWes played the piano during his run of <em>New Super Mario Bros. Wii</em>, using his head and feet to control the game via motion controls; and Dr. Doot did an <em>Elden Ring</em> bosses speedrun using an electric saxophone. Yes, the saxophone did make little <em>doot doot </em>sounds the entire time.</p>
