<p class="has-text-align-none">While fans eagerly anticipate <em>Grand Theft Auto 6</em>, players are still getting plenty of mileage out of the game’s predecessor. Rockstar is continuing to provide official updates for <em>GTA Online</em>, and players are building their own role-play servers set in San Andreas. These servers’ players build their own cultures, implementing homebrewed mechanics and new ways to role-play. One of these servers is <a href="https://purplerp.com/">PurpleRP, which recently launched a 1.0 update</a> that includes racing and boosting systems, housing and property, agriculture, drugs, and turf systems. The server currently hosts hundreds of players every day.</p>
