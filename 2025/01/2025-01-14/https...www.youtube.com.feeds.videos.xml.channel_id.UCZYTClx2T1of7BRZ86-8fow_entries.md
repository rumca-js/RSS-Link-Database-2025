## Does Underwear Affect Sperm Counts?

When it comes to things that affect sperm counts, there's a lot of rumors out there. Anything from diet and exercise to choice in underwear and even how you sit are all debated as possible behaviors that can cause an individual to accidentally lower sperm counts. So we decided to wade through the truth and the fiction to figure out once and for all what really hurts your swimmers, and what's just a myth.
