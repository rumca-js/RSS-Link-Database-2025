Suraya Mohamed | January 5, 2025
NMIXX is not just another South Korean girl group. These talented ladies are accomplished singers who performed this 26 minute set in one take and without sound reinforcement wedges that musicians traditionally use to hear themselves better. It’s not easy for any group, especially one with six vocalists, to move out of their comfort zone of a traditional recording environment, but NMIXX fully embraced the Tiny Desk ethos. Using the natural room acoustics to hear themselves and the band, the singers’ intonation and vocal execution was superb as they sang arrangements carefully prepared for this special setting.
