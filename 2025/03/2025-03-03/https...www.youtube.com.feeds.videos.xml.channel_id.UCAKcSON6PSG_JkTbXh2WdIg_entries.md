Following a session in The Current studio, singer-songwriter Gillian Welch talked about how a guitar she bought just after she released her first album has been her touring mainstay ever since. 

Here's what Welch had to say:

"I'm Gillian Welch, and this is my 1956 Gibson J-50. I've been playing it since we put out our first record [Revival, released in 1996]. There's a really good J-50.'
